Field Manual

Pick a weapon (or press 1–5), then click open terrain to deploy. Hold Shift while placing to deploy several in a row. Click a deployed weapon to upgrade damage, fire rate, or range, change its targeting priority, or sell it back for 70%.

Every kill pays a bounty and each cleared wave adds a cash bonus — but every wave hits harder. Runners arrive at wave 3, brutes at wave 5, and a boss every 5th wave.

Space starts the next wave. Right-click or Esc cancels placement. NEW MAP rolls a fresh road and biome — grasslands, desert, badlands, or tundra, all under cover of night.
